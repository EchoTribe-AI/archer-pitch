The Intelligence Stack
Four products. One closed loop.
Each product does one job precisely. Together they cover the full path from detecting what's working, to routing for maximum EPC, to creating content, to amplifying with paid spend — across any affiliate type in the network.
EchoSignal
Monitor & Detect
Watches every affiliate in the network — creator posts, publisher content pages, link click velocity — and surfaces which products and which pieces of content are outperforming right now. Updated every 48 hours. Flags amplification windows before they close.
Applies to: social creators + content publishers
EchoRoute
Route & Maximize EPC
Delivers the highest-EPC link for every product click — routing to the merchant with the best commission rate and conversion rate for that affiliate's specific audience. Not Amazon by default. The right merchant, determined by real CVR data.
Applies to: any affiliate with multi-merchant options
EchoAgent
Create & Engage
Curates product collages and shoppable posts from EchoSignal's top-performing products, ready to publish. Also engages audiences in real time — answering product questions with affiliate-linked recommendations. Every answer is a monetized touchpoint.
Applies to: social creators + publisher content pages
EchoBoost
Amplify & Convert
Uses EchoSignal's top content + EchoRoute's top merchant to identify the exact moment and product to amplify — then enables brands or affiliates to build ads and push them into Meta, TikTok, and beyond. Signal, routing, and spend all in one motion.
Applies to: brands funding campaigns + affiliates self-boosting
