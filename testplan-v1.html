Attribution
How we track every click and sale
The click flow — Meta ad → Amazon purchase
Step 1
Meta ad fires — user sees boosted post, clicks. Facebook click ID (fbclid) captured in URL.
Step 2
Landing page — user lands on shoppable Mommy & Me page. fbclid stored. Facebook Pixel fires PageView + ViewContent. First-party data captured.
Step 3
Product click — user clicks Shop Now → Archer attribution link fires → routes to Amazon. 14-day cookie set.
Step 4
Purchase — any purchase of the Archer brand product within 14 days attributed. Commission lands in Archer's account.
Step 5
Reporting — Archer's /insights API returns clicks, conversions, new-to-brand purchases, brand halo sales, and add-to-cart data per ASIN per campaign label. Weekly report generated automatically.
Important: Each ad variant gets a unique Archer attribution link with a distinct campaign label (e.g. steph-apr1-var-a). This enables split testing performance by creative, by routing (landing page vs. direct), and by product — all trackable separately in Archer's reporting.
